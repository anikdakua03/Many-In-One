Some Overviews

  1. 1. This will be a time bounded quiz .
  2. 2. Questions are based on some general category or user created category.
  3. 3. Questions are either true / false or single correct options or multiple correct option or any of them .
  4. 4. Questions will have easy, medium , hard level difficulty .
  5. 5. You can create quiz with questions from 5 - 50 [ multiple of 5] .
  6. 6. You can navigate to other questions before/after answering while quiz is running.
  7. 7. If you can navigate to other pages while the quiz is running, all progress will be lost .
  8. 8. You can check your score with some other measures after submitting the quiz .
